Our Mission

War leaves wounds the eye cannot see. We exist to heal them — with rigor, reverence, and the long patience that recovery requires.

The War Survivors Institute is an independent nonprofit dedicated to the treatment of trauma caused by armed conflict. Drawing on more than twenty years of clinical practice and peer-reviewed research, we serve those most often overlooked — and train the clinicians, communities, and institutions around them.

Who We Serve

Three populations. One shared wound.

Our programs are tailored to the distinct experiences of each community we serve — yet built on the same foundation of dignity, evidence, and time.

01 — Veterans

For those who served.

Combat veterans carry injuries that don't always bleed. We offer integrated care for PTSD, moral injury, and the quiet aftershocks of deployment — in partnership with the VA and community providers.

Learn more

02 — Civilians

For those who endured.

Civilians — children, elders, entire neighborhoods — bear war's heaviest weight. We bring trauma-informed care and community-based support to those who lived under siege, occupation, or displacement.

Learn more

03 — Refugees

For those who fled.

Resettlement does not end the war inside. We work alongside refugee families navigating grief, loss, and the long road to feeling at home — in fifteen countries and counting.

Learn more

Our Approach

Healing. Connecting. Educating.

Three pillars, drawn from twenty years of clinical practice, hold up everything we do. Each is necessary. None is sufficient alone.

01

Healing

Evidence-based clinical care for PTSD, complex trauma, moral injury, and grief — delivered with the time and humanity recovery requires.

02

Connecting

Peer community, family programs, and spaces where survivors recognize one another — because trauma isolates, and connection is medicine.

03

Educating

Clinician training, public scholarship, and policy work — so that what we learn at the bedside reaches every community that needs it.

Michael Hollifield, M.D.

Founder & Medical Director
